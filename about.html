Our Mission

Bridging Agricultural Communities Across India

From humble beginnings to becoming a nationwide agricultural commodities powerhouse

Who We Are

Established in 1971, Modasiya Brokers has grown from a small regional operation to one of India's most trusted agricultural commodities brokerage firms. With over five decades of experience, we have built an extensive network spanning all agricultural zones across the country.

Our deep-rooted connections with farming communities, distributors, and end consumers have positioned us uniquely to understand and fulfill the needs of India's diverse agricultural market.

Our Vision & Mission

Our vision is to create a sustainable and efficient agricultural ecosystem that benefits every stakeholder in the supply chain. We believe in empowering farmers by providing them fair access to national markets while ensuring consistent quality supplies to consumers and businesses.

Through our brokerage services, we aim to bridge the gap between agricultural producers and consumers, overcoming geographical, informational, and logistical barriers that have traditionally fragmented India's agricultural markets.

Our Story

Five Decades of Agricultural Excellence

Milestones that have shaped our journey in revolutionizing India's agricultural commodity trading

1971

Humble Beginnings

Founded by Mr. Saeed Ahmed in Himmatnagar, Gujarat as a small local broker connecting regional farmers with nearby markets, focusing initially on pulses trading.

1985

Regional Expansion

Expanded operations throughout Gujarat and neighboring states, establishing our first warehouse facilities and quality testing protocols to ensure consistent product standards.

1998

Diversification

Broadened our product portfolio to include grain seeds and cattle feed, becoming a comprehensive agricultural commodities broker catering to diverse farming needs.

2007

National Presence

Established offices in major agricultural hubs across India, creating a nationwide network that connects farmers from every region with national and international markets.

2015

Digital Transformation

Embraced technology to create digital platforms for market intelligence, price forecasting, and streamlined logistics, bringing transparency and efficiency to agricultural trading.

2025

Future Forward

Today, with 5000+ farmers in our network and partnerships with major agricultural businesses, we continue to innovate and expand our services while staying true to our founding principles of integrity, quality, and fair trade.
